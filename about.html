Our Story

Saman Traders was established in 2010 with a vision to bring high-quality, affordable products to the families of Kuliyapitiya. What started as a small corner shop has blossomed into a beloved community staple.

We pride ourselves on our selection. From the safest toys for your little ones to the daily essentials that keep your home running, every item on our shelves is chosen with care.
